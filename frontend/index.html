📖 About Us

Mission

To provide quality education that transforms lives and builds future leaders.

Vision

To be a center of academic excellence in Uganda and beyond.

History

Jinja Senior Secondary School was established in 1965 with a vision to provide quality education to the youth of Jinja and surrounding areas. Over the decades, we have grown into one of the most respected secondary schools in the region.
